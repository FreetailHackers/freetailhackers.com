Challenges

Best Novice Hack
New to hackathons? Feel like there is too much competition? We got you covered! This challenge is for teams where at least 2 members or, if you are competing solo and you are new to the scene. To qualify as a beginner, either Longhorn Hack for Humanity must be your first-ever hackathon **OR** you must have less than 1 year of programming experienc

Best Overall Hack
The ultimate winner of Longhorn Hack for Humanity!! This is the team that scores the highest on both Gavel and our judging rubric.

Best Human Rights Hack
In light of new policies and current events that threaten human rights worldwide, we’ve added this challenge geared towards a hack that best provides a solution or mechanism to improve the rights of an individual, group, or community. Some examples of sub-categories within this one include LGBTQ+ Rights, Accessibility, Security, Privacy, Tech Policy, and Healthcare Assistance, to name a few. We encourage you to deeply think about what it means to advocate for one or more of these causes and provide solutions that improve the quality of life of many.

Best Humanitarian Aid Hack
It’s not a surprise that the Russia-Ukraine crisis keeps everyone on their toes. Or, for that matter, any humanitarian crisis happening domestically or worldwide. In this challenge, we are looking for the best hack that provides humanitarian aid or assists in obtaining it. Sub-categories within this challenge include, but are not limited to: refugee and asylum assistance, addressing resource scarcity (food, water, health, access to medical care, education, shelter, etc.), war crises (Russia-Ukraine, Syria, Yemen, indigenous genocide, etc.), and tech for organizations that are trying to help (UNHCR, UNICEF, Red Cross, etc.).
