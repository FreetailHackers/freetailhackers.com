FAQ

How do teams work?
Teams can be up to 3 members. If you don't have a team, don’t worry! Joining a team of new friends is the best part of a hackathon. We will have team matching available for everyone on the day of the event, but if you would like to message our channel and create a team beforehand, that's okay too! We will have a form to fill out and confirm team information.

When does registration close?
Registration closes when the Hackathon starts! Because of the in-person setting, we want to open up the opportunity to as many people as possible, however do fill up spots on a first come first serve basis. We will open waitlists and allow for a limited number of walk-ins on the day of! We would love it if you could fill out the application as soon as possible to help us plan for the event :)

Will there be prizes and tracks?
Yes! We plan to give prizes to the top placing team in each category. More information on this will be available here.

Will there be food at the event?
Yes! We will provide lunch to all registered attendees (mindful of dietary restrictions and accommodations based on the registration form). We will also be providing specialty snack items around dinner and have a profit-share with Gong Cha Boba from 4 - 8PM (mention Freetail Hackers) that is also a part of the event!

Where will walk-in check-in be?
It will be on the 3rd floor bridge of the Gates Dell Computer Science Building (GDC 3.100)
